Winners of Build It with Nokia Lumia 1020 Hackathon

Check the Project website ---> Here for more.

Slide Puzzle is a windows phone application which was built during the two day hackathon help at Startup Sauna,Aalto University- Finland as part of "Build it with Nokia Lumia 2010" organized by AppCampus in collaboration with Nokia Research and Microsoft Research.


"Slide Puzzle" won the contest and the application will be available in windows market soon.

Enjoy Playing it and Train your brain.
